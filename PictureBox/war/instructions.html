Instructions for using Picture Box

You can sign in to flop box or use it without signing in as a guest. Guests can:

  • View or download images set as public by members

 Members, who can sign in with their Google account, can:

  • View or download all images uploaded to Flop Box
  • Upload images to Flop Box and choose to have them public or private
  • Delete any of the images they uploaded

Any images uploaded by members are then available to all users. If made public, they are available to anyone, whether or not they sign in. Admins may delete any images stored on Flop Box.


Once logged in just click upload and then select a file and press the upload button.


The file is then saved in the application and available any time you log in.


The file can be deleted by hitting the delete button.
